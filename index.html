Who We Are

We Make Strategy Simple and Powerful

Retela Strategic Intelligence is a division of Retela Business Group. We exist for one reason: to give leaders the insight they need to make better decisions — faster, with more confidence.

Our Vision

To build AI-driven intelligence tools that help any organisation — no matter the size — move smarter through a complex world. We want every leader to have access to the kind of insight once only available to the largest firms.

Our Mission

To deliver sharp, actionable insight through our DSIIR framework. We help clients act at the right time, in the right place, with the right information — turning uncertainty from a threat into an advantage.

Our Method

The DSIIR Framework

Dynamic Strategic Insights & Intelligence Reporting

DSIIR is not a dashboard or a news feed. It is a system that takes data and turns it into clear intelligence — the kind that helps you act before your competitors even know what's happening.

The 44 analyses we produced in Q1 2026 all came from public data. For us, the problem is never data. The problem is always methodology. That is what we solve.

Five Core Principles

01
